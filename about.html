I'm Sort Of New Here...

It's true: I am very inexperienced compared to come people at coding. Before you dismiss me, let me explain what I learned from my previous career.

As a tenacious ex-Assistant Producer from the TV world, I would often work solo, left to manage my own time whilst juggling multiple responsibilities and projects.

I am about to graduate from a Software Development Bootcamp with JustIT Training, having completed several projects to further develop my skills and promote independent research and learning. These projects are available on my portfolio and hopefully demonstrate my abilities in HTML, CSS, Python and JavaScript.

While I feel lucky to have worked such a fun industry, TV would always devour itself.

So i escaped while I could and I am grateful and excited to begin the transition to a career in Software Development. My love of technology and all things nerdy has helped me realise this is the start of something amazing. I hope to quickly start my new career as a Software Developer and learn as much as I can to aid my future development.
